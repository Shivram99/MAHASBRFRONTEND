Sanstha Aadhaar Number (SAN) is the unique ID given to all Govt. departments, Boards/ Corporation/ Autonomous Body/ Non-government organizations and all enterprises including private enterprises established in Rajasthan. Such a database when complete will be an integrated platform for all institutions and will be used for planning and monitoring by the government. All type of benefits /services/ subsidies shall be given by “Sanstha Aadhaar”..
